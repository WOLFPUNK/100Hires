# Organic growth performance often depends on systems that are easy to take for granted

Organic growth performance often depends on systems that are easy to take for granted - site architecture, tracking, indexing, and technical setup. 

When those systems break, the impact is rarely immediate or obvious, which makes the problem harder to catch.

Teams keep shipping, dashboards keep updating, and issues quietly compound in the background. 

This is especially pertinent as things move at lightning speed with AI. This makes small issues compound significantly faster, causing technical debt spirals and often the "mount AI" phenomenon we see as as a result of poor quality SEO/AEO programs. We're talking to many companies about cleanup jobs right now. 

This essay looks at how those failures happen and what teams can do to catch them early.
