# Even though clicks drop like flies when Google shows AI Overviews, 80% of searchers find their final answer from organic search results!

That headline comes from the first independent UX study of AIOs: 70 U.S. searchers, 400 live AIO encounters, every scroll, hover and "show more" coded frame-by-frame.

This study changed the way I look at SEO and AI.

Some highlights:
🔍 Seven in ten users never read beyond the top third of the AIO-> surface early or stay invisible

⚡ The new filter users apply to Search is "Do I trust this brand?" → "Does it answer my question?" -> authority now outranks relevance

🤔 Gen Z & millennials embrace AI Overviews, 55-plus stick to blue links

Clicks are melting faster than ice on asphalt; visibility is the new currency.

How to react: Shift KPIs from rank to visibility inside the AIO, fortify brand trust signals, and seed content on validation hubs like Reddit & YouTube before the traffic tide recedes.
