# The SEO job market has quietly repriced itself around AI skills. Job data from 946 full-time SEO roles shows companies are willing to pay a premium for AI skills.

The breakdown:
1. SEO jobs mentioning AI in the title pay $113,625 at the median, compared to $89,438 for those that don't.
2. That is a live 27% salary gap in the market right now.
3. The requirement is largely hidden: filtering jobs by title misses 80% of the AI-required roles.
4. The premium compounds with seniority, jumping significantly at the mid and management levels.

My Take: The data shows that the market expects senior leaders to be proficient in AI. As teams shrink or remain flat, the expectation is for everyone to leverage AI for better work.
