# Today 90% of my work as a CMO runs through Claude Code.

Today 90% of my work as a CMO runs through Claude Code. 
Here's everything AI handled for me just last week:

▪️ Analyzed my job interviews, spotted my weak patterns, and built an "interview prep" skill to nail the next ones.
▪️ Helped me draft a keynote for Ahrefs Evolve from a bunch of freeform dictations and links to reference material.
▪️ Drafted product positioning for a new product by analyzing a dozen similar ones and feeding in my team's Slack chatter about what we're building.
▪️ Analyzed my business call transcripts and helped clarify on a bunch of things that went over my head during the actual call.
▪️ Wrote a draft video ad script based on my raw idea in under 5 minutes.
▪️ Built a few new mini-apps and automations (see my earlier post for examples).
▪️ And this very post — dictated freestyle and polished by Claude, using a skill I built earlier.

Just six months ago, I was publicly trashing "vibe marketing" as a fad.

But look at me now. 😅 

In my defense, six months ago AI genuinely sucked at everything I needed it for. Not anymore.

And Claude Code isn't even built for marketers.

Now imagine what's possible with an AI agent designed specifically with marketers in mind. That's what we're building at Ahrefs with Agent A.

So expect my next post to start with "Here's what Agent A handled for me just last week." 😉
