# Introducing the Raptive SEO Workbook!

Introducing the Raptive SEO Workbook! There are plenty of SEO tools that tell you what happened. The hard part is turning that into what to do next. That's where the Raptive SEO Workbook comes in.

My team has been working hard to support the 6,300+ publishers in the Raptive network, collectively generating > 20B pageviews / year and today we're releasing the SEO Workbook - the only SEO tool that actually understands your publishing schedule and workflow.

Most SEO tools can show you performance data - but it's a struggle for most publishers to translate that into actionable insights. We combine 3rd party data from SEO tools as well as first party data like search console and wordpress data to give you a complete picture of performance.

The SEO Workbook is designed to help evergreen content publishers build and maintain their content library. With core insights around how your new content is performing and which pages you should be updating to maintain and grow existing rankings.

The workbook offers four key views:

1. The performance summary shows you the macro view of how your traffic is performing, alongside your publishing volume over time (new content and updated content). Publishers more active in publishing and updating content easily out-perform those that don't meet publishing benchmarks.

2. The pages to update report gives you a focused, prioritized list of pages that you should be updating. We know when every page was published and last modified so we can easily filter to pages that are old, are dropping in rankings and have traffic potential. (Bonus: we filter this by evergreen vs seasonal views of data to help you prioritize content updates around seasonal peaks)

3. The new pages report shows you the contribution of new content over time. This is the compound growth chart that shows how you're investing in content for future performance. I've wanted a view of this data ever and so we finally built it! No other SEO tool gives you this reliably that I'm aware of.

4. The all pages tab - this is a full table of the raw data for your site. While this might not seem like the most exciting page it's fun to export the full CSV and put it into chatgpt. The AI tools are getting very good at synthesizing data and giving actionable insights - but they need the raw context! And now with a single click you can export a clean CSV ready to use how you want and feed into LLM tools.

The SEO Workbook is available today for publishers in the Raptive network. If you're interested in a demo shoot me a message.

(Oh, and we're launching a similar workbook for Google Discover very soon too. We're building some cool stuff this year!)
